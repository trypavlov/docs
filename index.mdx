Trig helps B2B SaaS companies understand their customer journey, identify who needs attention, and intervene at exactly the right moment. One platform configuration enables pattern discovery, early warning, automated interventions, and impact measurement.

## How Trig Works

Trig connects your CRM and product data to build a complete picture of every customer's journey. By tracking progression through lifecycle stages, Trig establishes what "normal" looks like and identifies customers who are ahead or behind.

## Why Trig?

### Close the Gap Between Insight and Action

Most customer success tools tell you what's happening. Trig helps you do something about it. The insight that "47 customers haven't completed onboarding" becomes actionable when you can immediately target those customers with a relevant message.

### Timely, Contextual Intervention

The closer an intervention arrives to the moment of need, the more effective it is. Trig campaigns trigger based on real-time criteria, collapsing the gap between "customer needs help" and "customer receives help."

### One Configuration, Multiple Value Drivers

Without lifecycle stages, every analysis requires separate data work. With Trig's unified configuration, pattern discovery, early warning, automated interventions, and impact measurement all become possible on a shared foundation.
